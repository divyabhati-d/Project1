1. Fender could not play the guitar:

When you hear the name, Fender, you immediately recognize him as the founder of the guitar. But, what if I say that the founder of the instrument, Leo Fender, actually never played the instrument? He did not even learn to play the guitar. Fender started his career by repairing phonographs, radios, and other electronics. In the 1940s, Fender started manufacturing guitars as a different type of instruments. Later in the 1950s, they launched the first mass-produced electric guitar.

2. Music synchs with your heartbeats:

Experts say that music is directly linked to the activities of the different parts of our body, especially the cardiovascular system. In research, it was found earlier that hearts respond differently to different beats and tempos of music. Moreover, the brain cells also respond to music quickly. It also shows how a distorted piece of music can increase heartbeats and increase blood pressure.

3. Eminem- Rap God:

A hit song by Eminem holds the world record for having the most number of words contained in a song. The song, Rap God, holds 1560 words in the song. And he sang them at an average pace of 4.28 words each second. The song was released in the year 2013, in the month of October. It belonged to the album Marshal Matters.

5. Singers have a shorter life span than others:

According to the study conducted by the University of Sydney, singers and musicians have a shorter life span than others. Although there is no practical reason behind it, they have found it from the number of suicides, homicides, and accidental deaths. The data was studied based on the sex of the musicians and the decade of their death.

7. Some people are really insensitive to music:

A study conducted by the University at Barcelona showed that 5% of the total gathered people do not feel anything towards music. Although they can take pleasure from other things like food, sleep, travel, and others, they do not feel anything while listening to music. Moreover, these students were not found with any physical or mental disorders.

8. The world’s oldest song:

The Hurrian Hymn No.6is considered the world’s oldest song. It was sung about more than 3400 years ago. The song is said to be a rhyme of an ancient goddess Nikkai. It was composed in the 14th century. The tunes of the song were discovered in clay tablets. It was excavated in the 1950s.
